Research

Overview​

This section compiles key research, analyses, and technical insights relevant to Anarkoswap's design and the decentralized exchange (DEX) ecosystem. It serves as a resource for developers, researchers, and community members seeking a deeper understanding of the protocol and its underlying mechanisms.

Topics Covered​

1. Automated Market Maker (AMM) Models​

  • Overview of AMM mechanisms (constant product formula, concentrated liquidity)
  • Comparison of Uniswap v1, v2, and v3 architectures and innovations

2. Liquidity Provision Economics​

  • Capital efficiency of concentrated liquidity versus traditional liquidity
  • Impermanent loss analysis and mitigation strategies
  • Fee structure impacts on LP incentives

3. Swap Routing and Price Optimization​

  • Multi-version routing algorithms
  • Price impact and slippage management
  • Strategies for aggregating liquidity across versions

4. Security and Risk Analysis​

  • Smart contract audit summaries
  • Common vulnerabilities and mitigations in AMM protocols
  • Oracle manipulation and front-running considerations

5. Tokenomics and Incentive Models​

  • Design considerations for native reward tokens
  • Governance models and community incentives
  • Future token integration strategies

6. Market Trends and Comparative Studies​

  • DEX market growth and competition landscape
  • User behavior and liquidity distribution patterns
  • Emerging technologies in decentralized finance

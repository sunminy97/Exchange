Frequently Asked Questions

What is AnarkoDEX?

AnarkoDEX is a decentralized exchange (DEX) that allows users to swap tokens directly from their wallets using smart contracts, without intermediaries.

How do I use AnarkoDEX?

A. Visit the website → Connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask) → Select tokens → Enter the amount → Click “Swap” to execute the transaction.

Is AnarkoDEX secure?

AnarkoDEX operates on audited smart contracts and does not hold user funds. Your assets remain in your wallet at all times. We do not store private keys.

My assets are missing. What should I do?

AnarkoDEX does not custody funds. If assets appear missing, check your wallet and transaction status on a block explorer. Contact support if needed.

What are the fees on AnarkoDEX?

AnarkoDEX charges a small trading fee (e.g., 0.3%) per swap. Additionally, users pay network (gas) fees directly to the blockchain.

Why did my swap fail?

Swaps can fail due to slippage, low liquidity, or insufficient gas. Try increasing slippage tolerance or gas limit and try again.

Which wallets are supported?

We support MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, and other major EVM-compatible wallets.

Can I use AnarkoDEX on mobile?

Yes, you can access AnarkoDEX via mobile browser wallets or by connecting through WalletConnect.

Does AnarkoDEX have its own token?

An AnarkoDEX governance or utility token is under consideration. Any official announcement will be made via our verified channels.
